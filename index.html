How to get more apps on webOS Homebrew Channel?

Step 1. Make sure you have Homebrew Channel on your LG TV.

Step 2. Click the button.

Step 3. Click "Add Repository", and type the link included from this website, and press "ADD REPOSITORY".

Step 4. Click < button on the left side and voila, it show more apps now!
